services

"WHOLE PACKAGE" CLINIC ACCREDITATION

We can carry out “whole package” accreditation services, from the initial application to your clinic receiving Full Accreditation Award.

With over a dozen years of experience with compliance for diagnostic imaging and pain management clinics, we have a thorough understanding of what it takes to achieve full accreditation award for your facility. Our services include:

  • evidence submissions
  • documentation packages matching guidelines and standards (policy & procedure manual, checklists, clinic logs, agreements, etc)
  • patient facing pre/post procedure documentation

OUR MISSION IS TO MAKE STANDARDS COMPLIANCE A BYPRODUCT OF SAFE, EFFICIENT, AND CONTINUOUS HIGH-QUALITY CLINICAL PROCESSES
